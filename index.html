About

Patent Alert is an app designed to inform patients about medication patent expiration dates. This allows patients to acquire the necessary knowledge to to make informed decisions with their primary physician.


Developers: Maelle Vance, Nicholas Hunt-Walker, Tre Cain, Aliza Pilisuk, Shane Henning, Will Weatherford, and Munir Ibrahim.
